Max Temperature

In this plot, the Latitude of the location was placed on the X axis with the Max Temperature on the Y axis. We wanted to see if there was a correlation between the location's proximity to the equator and the temperature. It can be observed that there is indeed a strong relationship between the latitude and the max temperature. As the location gets closer to a latitude of 0(the equator), the max temperature rises and then falls again as it gets further away for a 0 latitude.
